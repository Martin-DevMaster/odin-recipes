Elk Steak

Marinated Elk Steak with Whiskey

his is such a flavorful recipe and is great enjoyed on the grill with a little advanced planning. Elk steaks don't really need anything and are great by themselves, but if you are looking to add some extra flavor, this marinade is sure to please!

Ingredients

  • 4 Elk Steaks
  • 1/2 Cup Whiskey
  • 1/2 Cup Brown Sugar

Steps

  1. Step 1: In a large bowl mix all the ingredients for the marinade together and pour into a large resealable bag. Add the Elk steaks and coat well. Seal the bag and refrigerate for 6-12 hours.
  2. Heat grill to medium heat and remove steaks from bag and discard marinade. We recommend no more than medium-rare, so 5-7 minutes per side maximum cooking time.
  3. Remove from grill and enjoy the beauty you've created.
