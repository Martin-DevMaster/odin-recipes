Pizza

Yummy Crusty Not Vegan Pizza

Amazing and tasty looking pizza with thin crust and sweet melting cheese.

The pepperoni, mushrooms and I assume pickles are just additional awesomeness of this magnificent thing

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cups bread flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp olive oil ((or any other oil))

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 250°C / 480°F (230°C fan) (Note 4.) Place shelf in the top quarter of the oven.
  2. Spray pizza pan or baking tray lightly with oil.
  3. Prepare pizza sauce and toppings. Keep shredded mozzarella in the fridge
